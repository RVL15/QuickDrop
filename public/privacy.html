Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026

QuickDrop is committed to protecting the privacy of its users. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding that information.

1. Data We Collect

  • Uploaded files: Files you choose to upload are temporarily stored on the server to enable transfer between devices.
  • Session metadata: We generate session identifiers and basic timestamps to manage transfers. No personal profile information is required.
  • Optional analytics: Aggregate, non-identifying metrics may be collected to improve service reliability.

2. How We Use Data

  • To facilitate file transfers between devices.
  • To debug and improve the service and detect abuse.
  • To comply with legal requests where required.

3. File Retention and Deletion

Files uploaded through QuickDrop are retained only as long as necessary to complete the transfer. By default, uploaded files are removed automatically after a short retention period (configurable on the server). Users are responsible for backing up any files they wish to retain.

4. Third-Party Services

QuickDrop may rely on third-party infrastructure (for example hosting or analytics). These providers are contractually required to protect your data and may have their own privacy policies.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect uploaded data in transit and at rest, but no internet-hosted service can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. Avoid uploading sensitive personal data unless you trust the receiving device.

6. Your Choices

You control which files are uploaded and when a session is active. If you need removal of retained files or have privacy concerns, contact the site administrator (see Contact page).
