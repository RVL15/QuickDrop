Privacy Policy

Your files are handled for transfer, not for tracking.

QuickDrop keeps the transfer process lightweight. Uploaded files are processed temporarily so they can move between devices, then removed according to the server's retention rules.

What we collect

  • Uploaded files: Stored temporarily to complete the transfer.
  • Session metadata: Short-lived session IDs and basic timestamps for routing.
  • Operational logs: Minimal technical logs for performance and troubleshooting.

How we use it

  • To connect the sender and receiver in the same session.
  • To monitor upload health and prevent abuse.
  • To improve reliability and resolve issues.

Retention and deletion

Files are retained only as long as needed to support the transfer flow. If you need a file removed sooner, contact the site owner. Users should avoid uploading sensitive content unless they trust the receiving device and hosting environment.

Third-party services

QuickDrop may run on hosting, tunnel, or analytics services. Those providers may process traffic or logs under their own policies. We aim to keep the transfer experience minimal and privacy-conscious.
